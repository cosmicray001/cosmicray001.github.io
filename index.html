Hi!

My name is Md Samiul Islam. I am a Software Engineer with a strong foundation in computer science and a focus on building scalable backend systems. My interests lie at the intersection of distributed systems, data engineering, information retrieval, and applied machine learning.

I am particularly interested in designing efficient architectures for real-world applications, optimizing system performance, and solving complex engineering challenges with clean, maintainable code.
